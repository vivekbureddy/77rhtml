Recipe List----Tea

    Ingredients

  • milk
  • water
  • tea powder
  • sugar
  • cardomom seeds
  • ginger

    Steps to prepare tea

  1. turn on the fire
  2. boil half cup water in a tea bownl
  3. add one small table spoon of tea powder
  4. add few cardomom seeds powder or seeds and the tiny chunks of ginger
  5. let it boil for 5 min
  6. then add half cup of milk to it and one table sppon of sugar
  7. stir it and boil it for one minute
  8. strain it and serve it in a cup
  9. tea is ready to serve
